CHUWI JAMES
Facilitator Learning & Mentorship Program

I was born and grew up in a slum. As a child, what crossed my mind as each day drew was to come up with a new and unique item ( a toy) that which I would proudly display to my friends in the play groups. Little did I know that whatever I was doing was a pathway to generational creativity and innovation skills. As life moved on, the skills were shuttered because I couldn't find a mentor and thus I lost hope. Nevertheless, the hope was raised when I came to know about WOKOBER. Thankfully, I am now in the team working to see that the children get inspired and mentored in their toys' making activities as we give them room to create, showcase, develop and contribute ingeniously to a better world.
