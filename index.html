Welcome to Wokober

We strive to nurture Learners with creative ability to their full potential to be creators, problem solvers and leaders, for a better world. We do these by leveraging the ingenuity of toy making as an approach to creative learning among children, in an environment that inspires wonder, innovation and collaboration, to propel them to greatness never imagined before.

Our story

Wokober was founded with the belief that; we can make the world a better place through conscious creation.

Our Mission

Elevating human experience through innovative designs, captivating storytelling and transformative education.

Featured Project

Toy Making & Innovation Festival 2023

The Wokober Toy & Innovation Festival 2023, was the second of its kind. It was a competitive event, aimed at promoting creativity, innovation and technology exploration within the communities. During the event 30-50 children who exhibit brilliance, potential and passion are identi ed and awarded in three categories (3-7, 8-12 and 13-17 years).

Featured Cause

Mentorship Programme 2023

This is a project based 1 year learning program that starts with study tours to expose the learners to di erent spheres of creativity, global trends in innovation, processes, tools, technology and many others. The study tours is followed by series of personal projects selected by the learners that nurtures imagination, critical thinking, practical exploration, real world application and self-development.

Featured Cause

Pavilion Design Competition 2023

This is a project design competition where pavilion designs are submitted in by different architect individuals or team of indiviuals. The pavillion is an important integral part of the festival experience and winning design is implemented. The team that won the design competition worked with Wokober team and other fabricators to fabricate/build and set up the pavilion.

Partner or Volunteer with us today

Education is the key that unlocks the golden door to freedom.”- George Washington Carver. This journey of discovery and mentorship of underprivileged children with great potentials within underserved communities of war stricken Northern Uganda, needs as many hands as possible in efforts to multiply the impact of our programs on the lives of the children and communities we serve.

How?

  • Cooperate Social Responsibility towards Education by your Company.
  • Grants from organizations passionate about Education Transformation, access and equity.
  • Personal or group in-kind donations to the program activities.
  • Becoming a program good will ambassador in your area, company, organization, city or country.
  • Come to Uganda and volunteer through exchange of your valuable skills and experiences with the children and communities we serve for a specific period of time. For international exchange volunteers, you can always reach out to us directly or through AIESEC in Uganda.

Oringa Jimmy
